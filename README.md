🌿 Grama Sanjeevini (ಗ್ರಾಮ ಸಂಜೀವಿನಿ)
Empowering Rural Artisans with Digital Intelligence
Grama Sanjeevini is a specialized Business-to-Business (B2B) and Analytics platform designed for rural entrepreneurs. It bridges the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern market intelligence, specifically focusing on the Hasta-Kala (Handicraft) Shop ecosystem.

🚀 Key "Elite" Features
📊 Micro-Sales Analytics
The heart of the app is a powerful data engine that helps artisans understand their business:

Best Seller Identification: Automatically highlights products that are moving fast so artisans can increase production.

Dead Stock Elimination: Flags items that haven't sold in 60+ days, helping shop owners clear inventory and save costs.

Profit Tracking: Real-time calculation of margins to ensure sustainable growth.

🎨 Premium User Experience
Modern Branding: Featuring a professional deep-blue theme and a high-fidelity heart icon symbolizing the passion behind every handmade product.

Artisan Dashboard: A simplified, icon-driven interface designed for ease of use in rural settings.

🛠 Tech Stack
Platform: Android (Java/Kotlin)

Architecture: MVVM for scalable and clean code.

Data Engine: Local SQLite/Room for offline-first performance in areas with low connectivity.
